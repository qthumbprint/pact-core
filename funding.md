The local citizen collectives own the physical hardware and hold exclusive possession of the node's private cryptographic signing keys. If a centralized authority tries to manipulate a local node, the steward simply kills the process, or the PACT network's multi-sigma threshold filter automatically identifies, isolates, and drops the corrupted data instantly.
